About Library

The library of SCHOOL OF COMPUTER SCIENCE is the oldest and largest academic library in the Khandesh region of North Maharashtra since June 1962 With a legacy that is close to 54 years, Library is the Soul of the institute. Teaching and learning system are supported by library through reading material.
